Our History

About Islamic Center

Masjid E Saara also known as Saara Masjid is located in Rayasandra, Bangalore. The foundation stone for this Masjid was laid in the year 2010. Initially, the space purchased was enough to perform daily prayers. And, there was almost no masjid in around 10km radius. Gradually, the need for more space raised as the development in and around the location peaked.

Offered Services

Islamic Classes

Join our comprehensive Islamic classes to deepen your understanding of the Quran, Hadith, and Islamic principles under the guidance of experienced scholars, fostering spiritual growth and knowledge enrichment.

Community Development Programs

Engage in our community development initiatives aimed at empowering individuals, fostering unity, and addressing societal needs through educational workshops, skill-building sessions, and awareness campaigns.

Mosque Development

Support our mosque development efforts aimed at expanding our facilities, enhancing infrastructure, and creating a welcoming environment for worshippers, ensuring a conducive space for spiritual growth and communal gatherings.

Helps Poor

Join hands with us in our mission to alleviate poverty by providing essential assistance to the underprivileged through food distribution drives, financial aid programs, and vocational training initiatives, making a tangible difference in the lives of those in need.
